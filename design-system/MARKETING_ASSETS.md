# Aquahealth Marketing & Brand Collateral Guidelines

This document details the brand application guidelines for offline collateral, marketing assets, technician uniforms, delivery vehicles, and social media grids. Consistent application across these touchpoints ensures Aquahealth establishes trust and premium positioning in Chennai.

---

## 1. Business Stationery

To convey a premium and technology-inspired service standard, all physical correspondence must use clean margins and high-quality print stock.

### 1.1 Letterhead & Communications
- **Stock**: 120gsm Executive Bond paper, Matte Bright White.
- **Header**: Logotype positioned in the top-right corner with 30px clear margins.
- **Footer**: Service hotline, email, registered office address, and copyright text printed in caption sizing (`#6B7280`) in a 3-column horizontal footer grid at the bottom.
- **Accents**: A single thin vertical border line in Bright Aqua (`#00A9CE`, 2pt width) running along the left margin of the page.

### 1.2 Business Cards
- **Dimensions**: Standard ISO `85.60 mm x 53.98 mm`.
- **Card Stock**: 400gsm Premium Linen texture paper or Velvet Touch Matte paper.
- **Front Side**:
  - Full solid Deep Ocean Blue background (`#0B4F8C`).
  - Centered all-white monochrome Aquahealth logomark.
- **Reverse Side**:
  - Clean white background (`#FFFFFF`).
  - Top-left: Miniature logotype lockup.
  - Bottom-left: Name, Title (e.g., "Senior Purifier Engineer"), Phone, and Email set in Charcoal Dark (`#111827`).
  - Bottom-right: Tiny QR code linking directly to the instant WhatsApp booking portal.

### 1.3 Service Invoices & Receipts
- Invoices must be clean and highly legible, structured with clear section grids.
- **Top Panel**: Aquahealth logo, Unique Invoice ID, Date, and Technician ID.
- **Main Section**: Clean grid table containing itemized columns (Item, Description, Unit Price, Tax, Subtotal).
- **Payment Box**: A dedicated block on the bottom-right showing a clean UPI QR Code for contactless payments, with a caption saying `"Scan using GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm to pay instantly."`
- **Bottom Disclaimer**: `"All premium parts replaced come with a standard 90-day service warranty."`

## 2. Technician Uniform Guidelines

Aquahealth field engineers are the face of our brand in Chennai kitchens. Their uniform must communicate hygiene, trust, and engineering competence.

### 2.1 Uniform Elements
- **Polo Shirt**:
  - Color: Solid Deep Ocean Blue (`#0B4F8C`).
  - Accents: Bright Aqua (`#00A9CE`) collar trim and sleeve cuffs.
  - Logo: Aquahealth primary logo embroidered on the left chest (width: `70px`).
- **Trousers**: Clean, tailored grey or navy chinos (dark cargo shorts or faded jeans are strictly prohibited).
- **Service Cap**:
  - Color: Deep Ocean Blue.
  - Front panel: Embroidered all-white logomark.
- **Footwear**: Clean, closed-toe black work shoes or clean safety sneakers (no open sandals).

### 2.2 Technician ID Badge
- Every engineer must wear an active laminated ID badge clipped to their chest.
- **Front Side**:
  - High-resolution headshot.
  - Name, Designation (e.g., "Certified Water Technologist"), and Technician Code.
  - Large green tick badge symbol displaying: `"Vetted & Background Checked"`.
  - Emergency Blood Group indicator on the bottom right.

## 3. Vehicle Branding (Service Vans & Bikes)

Aquahealth delivery and emergency service vehicles are mobile billboards. They must look clean, modern, and high-tech.

### 3.1 Service Van Wrap
- **Base Vehicle Color**: Pearl White or Solid Gloss White.
- **Sides Graphics**:
  - Large Aquahealth primary logo centered on the side panel (width: `1200px`).
  - Large tagline printed beneath the logo in Geist Bold: `"Pure Water. Honest Service."`
  - A clean, modern wave graphic sweeping from the rear wheel arch toward the front doors in transparent Bright Aqua gradient (`#00A9CE` to transparent).
- **Rear Doors**:
  - Hotlines and bookings: `"Book RO Service on WhatsApp: +91 98402 75122"`.
  - Clean checklist of services supported: `Sales • Installation • Filter Change • Repairs • AMC`.

## 4. Social Media Design Grids

All digital banners for Instagram, Facebook, and local advertisements must prioritize whitespace and typography over cluttered stock frames.

### 4.1 Grid Framework
- **Primary Layout Ratio**: 1:1 (Square, 1080px) or 4:5 (Portrait, 1080px x 1350px).
- **Whitespace Rule**: A minimum of 30% of the canvas area must be reserved for pure background to maintain premium Stripe/Linear layouts.
- **Typography hierarchy**:
  - Max 3 font weights per graphic.
  - Headings must use Geist Extra Bold. Caption notes should use Geist Mono for a technical, precise feel.
- **Image Filters**: Use sharp, crisp, high-contrast images. No dark vignettes, loud red discount circles, or yellow starbursts. Keep all promotional badges styled in clean, rounded-corner capsules.
